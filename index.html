The Witch's Grip

Orquette’s aquarium is in chaos—and not the fun kind. A mysterious witch has turned her watery sanctuary upside down, and it’s up to her to fix it. That means running, dashing, slashing through swarms of unruly lophiiformes, and chasing down the truth… even if it’s a little slippery.

You’ll play as Orquette herself, with each step closer to the truth unlocks juicy new illustrations—because of course, it wouldn’t be a JestersClubDev game without that signature art and animation flair, all crafted by the one and only Sumokuu.
And yes, Vasco Sanchez is back again with a killer soundtrack. STAY TUNED!

Orquette's Maze Adventure

Once home to a majestic octopus who tragically lost his tentacles in the mysterious mazes scattered throughout the aquarium, Orquette, the aquarium owner, now awaits a hero to restore what was lost.

Solve the mazes, each more challenging than the last. For every maze you solve, you retrieve a lost tentacle. Orquette rewards your efforts with playful and slightly naughty interactions that will leave you eager for more.

Girls on Cam

Orquette has her eye on someone—someone special, someone dazzling. But she can't quite bring herself to make the first move with her. So, she turns to you, her clever, witty confidant.

Here’s how it works: you’ll chat through messages, and every time you guide her just right, things heat up a little. She might even show you a little something if you’re clever enough. Who are we?

JestersClub.DEV is an independent video game studio founded in 2024 and based in Lyon, France. It was created with the idea of developing short games within the same universe, allowing players to discover characters and their stories throughout the gameplay.
As we started from publishing Orquette maze adventure as a joke to many, the success and devotion of our community pushed the developer to create the studio and expend the universe.
Currently composed of three team members, Our games are designed to be accessible while bringing a touch of nostalgia through both game mechanics and graphics.
If you've made it far enough to this section of the website, I hope you'll stay long enough to watch the studio grow ;)
